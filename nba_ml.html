NBA Machine Learning Model Design

Three different machine learning models were chosen to predict scores:

  • Gradient Boosted Tree (G.B)
  • Principal Component Analysis (PCA)
  • Random Forest (R.F)

    • All models were trained on roughly 5000 games from the past 6 years, exlcuding games that occured in the 2020-2021 season because of COVID-19's big impact on the sport. A multitiude of varibles were created and carefully trimmed down to roughly 12 variables. A majority of the variables represent the offensive part of the basketball teams, however there are several defense factors included. The model's does not factor in injuries, they assume each team is performing at its average performance so far this year. A full list of all factors that the models consider is below:

    Model Variables
    Team Average 2-Point Percentage
    Team Average 3-Point Percentage
    Team Average Free Throw Percentage
    Team Average Offensive Rebounds
    Team Average Defensive Rebounds
    Team Average Steals
    Team Average Blocks
    Team Average Turnovers
    Average Shot Attempts per Game
    Opponent Average Turnovers
    Opponent Average Blocks
    Opponent Average Steals
